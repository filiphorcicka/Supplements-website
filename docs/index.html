Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about protein and creatine supplementation

Is creatine safe to use long term?

Yes, creatine is one of the most well-researched and safe supplements available. When used at recommended doses, it’s safe for long-term use and may even offer benefits beyond fitness, like cognitive support and muscle preservation during aging.

Are supplements necessary if I eat a balanced diet?

While a balanced diet should always be your foundation, supplements can help fill nutritional gaps and provide concentrated nutrients during specific timing windows that benefit training. They're not essential but can be valuable tools for convenience and optimization.

Will protein powder make me bulky?

No, protein powder alone will not make you bulky. Muscle growth requires a combination of consistent strength training and a calorie surplus. Protein powder is just a convenient way to meet your protein needs.

Should I take creatine on rest days?

Yes, it is recommended to take creatine daily, even on rest days,to maintain elevated levels in your muscles. Consistency is key for maximizing its performance and recovery benefits.
